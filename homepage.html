Dr. Loughney graduated from the Loyola Stritch School of Medicine in Chicago. She completed her internal medicine internship and residency at the Walter Reed Army Medical Center in Washington, D.C. and was then deployed in support of Operation Desert Storm.

She returned to complete her fellowship in endocrinology at Walter Reed and served as an endocrinologist at Ft. Belvoir in Virginia. She has also been on the faculty of the John H. Burns School of Medicine at the University of Hawaii and later a staff endocrinologist at Walter Reed Army Medical Center. She has spent the last 12 years on the teaching staff in the endocrinology department at the Washington Hospital Center.

She is board certified by the American Board of Internal Medicine in Endocrinology, Diabetes, and Metabolism.
